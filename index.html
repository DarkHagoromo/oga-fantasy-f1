League Rules

🏁 Game Rules
  • Entry fee into the league is £15 each. Bank details provided by the OGA admin team.
  • Prediction for Season World Champion and Constructor must be submitted by EOD 14/03/2025. Once submitted, locked in permanently.
  • All race predictions must be provided before race qualifying starts. Predictions can be kept private until results are revealed.
  • Any late predictions will be reviewed and judged by the OGA (Ola Governing Association).
🏆 Points Scoring
  • Race weekends have a maximum of 5 points available.
  • 1 point for each driver correctly predicted in positions 1, 2, 3, and 4.
  • 1 point for correctly predicting the Fastest Lap driver.
